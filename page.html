Our Story

We’ve gathered an unconventional team of developers from all backgrounds – a former teacher, a college student, a banker and a pizza delivery guy to create a powerful platform that uses the integration of social media and celebrations to deliver a high- quality, curated celebration experience.

Our Vision

A one stop platform for gifts, cakes, reservations and more - curated with the help of artificial intelligence and social media. With just a few clicks, customers can have flowers and other gifts delivered to right their door hours before they leave for the restaurant they've made reservations for. All from our app.

Our Technology

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning automate and simplify the delivery of highly curated, personalized gifts and experiences right to the ones you love.
